Valence:The dimension of valence is the negative-positive valence of emotionand ranges from unpleasant to pleasant (unhappy-happy).


Arousal: Physiological arousal is the activation state of emotion, which ranges from inactive to active state


Dominance: A person's feeling of dominance in a situation is based on the extent to which he feels unrestricted or free to act in a variey of ways. For example, imagine yourself sweating in the exam hall due to unpreparedness. On the contrary, you are well prepared for the exam.
